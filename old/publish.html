A little about me

I am an aspiring programmer with my sights set on being either a Web Developer or a Software Engineer.

When I finished high school in 2012 I had no idea what I wanted to be or what I wanted to do, I only knew I wanted to get on with it. After trying out a programming fundamentals elective in my first year of university I had found my calling. I transferred into a Games Development course ('cause what could be cooler?), and now here I am! With only one subject left in my Games Dev course at UTS I currently have lots of time on my hands which I am using to develop this portfolio and look for work.

I really enjoy programming. I enjoy solving puzzles, experiementing, and working within systems which require precision and make sense.
